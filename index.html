·GAME DESIGN·

·Anti-Clockwise·

· Anti-Clockwise is a horror/romance visual novel centered around the concept of time travel. Transitioning from high school to university is challenging enough, but what unfolds here is far beyond what anyone could expect. In the game, you’ll meet new faces as well as familiar ones, and from your character’s perspective, you’ll decide who will accompany you on this unique journey. With such an unusual ability at your disposal, what could possibly go wrong?

· As the player, you’ll have the freedom to shape your own path, making choices that lead to a variety of different endings. ·


· The game will eventually include romance-driven storylines as the narrative progresses. ·


· Note: The game contains horror elements that may be disturbing to some players. ·


· Upon full release, the game is planned to include six unique story routes, each centered on a different character with their own personality and distinct endings. Every route will feature at least two possible conclusions. ·

WARNING

Although the game is currently in its demo phase, future builds may include disturbing and emotionally intense content that may not be suitable for everyone. Please consider your mental well-being and exercise discretion. Viewer discretion is advised. Thank you, and enjoy the experience!

About Me :

My name is Christopher Makakis, though I also go by the nickname Kenny. I am a graphic designer, indie game developer, photographer, and writer with a keen attention to detail. A true jack-of-all-trades, I can bring your ideas to life. If you need it, there’s a good chance I can make it happen. From character design to art creation, I have the skills to bring most of your creative visions to reality.

With a mindset of "If they can do it, so can I," nothing stops me from pursuing excellence in everything that captures my interest.
