·GAME DESIGN·

·Anti-Clockwise·

· Anti-Clockwise Is a horror/romance based Visual-Novel revolved around the concept of time-travel. Going from high-school to university can be hard but what happens is now here near what was expected. In the game you'll be able to meet new and old faces from you characters perspective and decide who will be the ones that will keep you company throughout this journey. With you having such a strange ability in use, what can go wrong?

· As a player, you get to choose your own path to follow and make your own unique choices that will lead to all kind of different endings.·

· The game will contain romance based routes in the feature as the story develops.·

· The game has horror based factors and might disturb some viewers. ·

· Upon full release the game is planed to have 6 routes with 6 different characters that posses their own unique personality and endings with each route having at least 2 endings. ·

WARNING

Although the game is for now a demo later builds may be a disturbing and emotionally intense experience that may not appeal to everyone. Take this in to account and put your mental well-being first. Viewer discretion is advised. Thank you and have fun!
